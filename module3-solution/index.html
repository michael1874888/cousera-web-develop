Our Menu
Chicken

Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure with our Chicken Delight, a sumptuous feast for the senses that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. Our tender, slow-roasted chicken is marinated in a mouth-watering blend of herbs and spices, ensuring that each bite is infused with flavor. To further elevate your dining experience, choose between our zesty lemon-herb sauce, bursting with tangy citrus notes, or our creamy garlic parmesan sauce, offering a rich and indulgent twist. Our Chicken Delight is served with a generous helping of fluffy garlic mashed potatoes, prepared with real butter and cream to create a smooth, velvety texture. To balance out this indulgent dish, enjoy a colorful medley of seasonal vegetables, steamed to perfection and lightly seasoned with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. As a finishing touch, we offer a choice of warm, freshly baked dinner rolls or a rustic slice of artisanal bread, perfect for soaking up every last drop of sauce. Designed with chicken enthusiasts in mind, our Chicken Delight promises a memorable and satisfying dining experience that will have you returning time and time again.

Beef

Prepare to be transported to culinary heaven with our Beef Extravaganza, a luxuriously indulgent dish that will delight even the most discerning of palates. Our prime cuts of juicy, tender beef are slow-cooked to perfection, with each morsel of meat braised in a rich and savory red wine reduction, allowing the flavors to meld together and create a truly unforgettable taste sensation. As the perfect accompaniment to our Beef Extravaganza, savor the taste of our medley of roasted root vegetables, including earthy beets, sweet carrots, and robust parsnips. Each vegetable is oven-roasted to bring out its natural sweetness, then drizzled with a hint of rosemary-infused olive oil for an extra layer of flavor. To complete this decadent dining experience, indulge in our signature horseradish cream sauce, which provides a burst of tangy and zesty flavors that perfectly complement the rich, succulent beef. Served with your choice of creamy, buttery polenta or fragrant basmati rice, our Beef Extravaganza is a dish that promises to leave you feeling thoroughly satisfied and eager to return for more.

Sushi

Welcome to the world of our Sushi Symphony, a gastronomic journey that will transport you to the heart of Japan with its harmonious blend of flavors, textures, and artful presentation. Our delectable assortment of fresh, melt-in-your-mouth sushi and sashimi creations showcases the very best of traditional Japanese cuisine, with each piece meticulously crafted by our skilled sushi chef to ensure an unforgettable dining experience. Our Sushi Symphony platter features an array of beloved favorites, such as delicate tuna and buttery salmon nigiri, as well as inventive and modern rolls that incorporate crispy tempura, creamy avocado, and succulent eel. In addition, our chef has carefully curated a selection of sashimi, offering a taste of the ocean with each slice of expertly prepared, melt-in-your-mouth fish. To enhance the flavors of our sushi and sashimi, we provide traditional accompaniments, such as zesty pickled ginger, fiery wasabi, and savory soy sauce. Our Sushi Symphony also includes a refreshing seaweed salad, lightly dressed with a tangy sesame vinaigrette, and a steaming bowl of miso soup, which features silken tofu, wakame seaweed, and green onions in a savory, umami-rich broth.
